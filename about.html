  • Audience

    Audience

    Anchored News aims to reach an audience of curious readers. The curious audience has yet to discover other news outlets outside of their immediate scope, which might even challenge dominant narratives.

  • Objective

    Outcome

    The objective of Anchored News is to enable audiences with knowledgeable conversations about global topics. It is the audience who initially selects what they believe to be worthy of inclusion in the domain. Journalists seeking inclusion will most often write about the topics which are currently popular.

  • Implications

    People's Impact on Journalism

    The ideals of Internet users are most heavily influenced by their exposure to media content of a certain nature. Within journalism, props such as headlines containing `buzzwords` are used to grab the attention of their intended audiences. Journalist commentary is also posed to accommodate for the mindset of its target audience.

    Media is influenced and consumed by internet users each second, leading to the formation of mainstream Internet cultures. In other words, a conglomerate of global cultures reactively influencing one another. It is apparent that human society has been accommodating its technology with a multitude of different, overlapping, and sometimes even conflicting cultures, each coexisting on diverse multimedia platforms

    Anchored News intends to approach these topics with a neutral stance. This can be accomplished by posing diverse content to be easily discoverable. Anchored News aims to provide the ability to effectively and intuitively compare content with open-ended filtering strategies and an effectively simple page layouts.
